# PriceForge - Current Features

PriceForge is an AI-powered platform for professionals to create, manage, and share structured price lists.

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. AI Price List Generation
- **Intelligent Creation**: Generate realistic pricing packages based on your profession, experience, and target market.
- **Service Breakdown**: Automatically suggests included services, add-ons, and stand-alone services (upsells).
- **Currency Support**: Automatic local currency detection based on your region.

### 2. Professional Profiles
- **Profile Management**: Maintain multiple professional identities.
- **Skill Levels**: Categorize your expertise (Beginner to Expert).
- **Target Markets**: Define your audience (Budget, Standard, Premium).
- **Contact Info**: Add public email and phone number for potential clients.
- **Portfolio Integration**: Showcase your external portfolio link.

### 3. Price List Editor
- **Full Customization**: Edit titles, summaries, package names, prices, and service lists.
- **Dynamic Controls**: Toggle public/private visibility instantly.
- **One-Click Deletion**: Easily remove old or experimental price lists and profiles.

### 4. Public Sharing
- **Shareable Links**: Beautiful, mobile-responsive public pages for your clients.
- **Professional Branding**: Your name and portfolio link can be displayed on public lists.
- **Quick Copy**: One-click "Copy Link" for easy sharing.

### 5. Security & Persistence
- **Secure Authentication**: User accounts and data protection via Supabase.
- **Real-time Updates**: Changes are saved and reflected immediately.
- **State Management**: Fast and fluid UI powered by Zustand.

## 🎨 Design & UX
- **Modern UI**: Dark-themed, glassmorphic design with premium aesthetics.
- **Seamless Transitions**: Powered by Framer Motion for a high-end feel.
- **Responsive Layout**: Works flawlessly on desktop and mobile devices.
- **Tailwind CSS Powered**: Easily customizable styles for developers.
